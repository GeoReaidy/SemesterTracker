Getting started
1. Launch Semester Tracker from the Start Menu or desktop shortcut.
2. Create a local account.
3. Enter the total number of credits required for your degree.
4. Add your semesters, courses, assignments, grades, and deadlines.
5. Configure deadline reminders from the Settings page.

Important notes
- Your data is stored locally on this computer.
- Keep your password safe. Password recovery is not currently available.
- Deadline notifications can be enabled or disabled in Settings.
- The Calendar page shows assignments that have a due date.
- For accurate GPA and CGPA results, make sure course credits, assignment
  weights, and grades are entered correctly.

Project information
Semester Tracker is an open-source C++17 desktop application built with Qt,
SQLite, and libsodium.
